Hotels

1) University accommodation

The ESUG-arranged conference accommodation deadline is now over. However you can book university rooms at a range of sites via this link.

  • The ESUG-booked roms are in Trinity Hall's Wychfield site (Point B in this map). Going from this accommodation to the venue (the Computer Laboratory) is 15-20 minutes by walking and 5 minutes on a bike. For access to and from Cambridge centre and the railway station, see this map.
  • If Wychfield is full when you consult the above link, rooms at other university sites may be adequately placed and/or adequately costed for your requirements. (For example, Churchill College is 0.75 miles (1.25 km) away from the venue and costs 65 pound per night.)

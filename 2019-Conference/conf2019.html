2019

The ESUG board is pleased to announce that the 27th ESUG conference/summer-school will be held in Cologne, Germany 26-30 August 2019 (with Camp Smalltalk 24/25). The conference is co-organised by ZWEIDENKER.

Conference prices in Euros:

  • Early Registration Fee: 500€ (all days) / 180€ (per day)
